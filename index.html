We've written the book "Introduction into the Theory of Algorithms and Data Structures" based on the first semester of our course "Algorithms and Data Structures" at Yandex School of Data Analysis. Here you can see its cover on the left. The book proved to be popular among the School current and former students. It was expected, as the material of our course is comparable and even more advanced than the material of the Algorithms courses from Stanford and Princeton which available at Coursera. There are also many algorithms that are mostly used at programming contests, but were not published and analysed in any of the popular books on algorithms. We didn't have enough time and motivation to put a lot of those in our first book. You can also see that it was only published in Russian. And it only covers the first half of our course.

So we have a plan to publish a book based on the whole course, include all the "black magic" from so-called "sports programming" in it and publish it in English. We were told that it will be possible to publish it in Springer if we do the book. We have prepared some materials to make it easier to write the second part of the book with the help of one of our students. So we are ready to start writing an amazing book for you, we just want to make sure that you really need this book and that our hard work is worth doing. We believe that only a monetary investment can show strong desire to get the book. On the other hand, a small fee of $30 shouldn't be too high to express the desire and to get a copy of the book, so we are waiting for your backing.
